# Retail-Analytics-Walmart-Case-Study
Analysis of Walmart’s five-year transactional data to derive insights on customer behaviour, sales trends, product performance, and efficiency.
Objective: This project seeks to analyze Walmart’s transactional data from the past five years to generate actionable insights into customer behavior, sales trends, product performance, and operational efficiency.

Business Problem : The problem is to analyze Walmart’s historical transactional dataset covering customer, product, and logistics information; in order to generate insights that improve profitability, enhance customer satisfaction, and streamline operational efficiency.

Data set: The dataset contains five years of Walmart transactions, including customer demographics, order details, product attributes, and sales logistics.
